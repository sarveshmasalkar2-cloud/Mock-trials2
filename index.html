Module A
Theme & Theory
▸

Theme = A single moral sentence the jury remembers. "They chose profit over people."

Theory = Your factual narrative of exactly what happened. It must explain every piece of evidence without contradiction.

This Case: Theme = Cost-cutting destroyed safety. Theory = Apex approved the bolt substitution knowing it failed quartering wind specs; Metro executed it for schedule bonuses.
Module B
Primacy / Recency
▸

Primacy = Jurors remember what they hear first. Lead with your strongest, most emotional point in every examination.

Recency = Jurors remember what they hear last. End every direct and cross on a high note — never bury your best point in the middle.

Application: Open Dr. Hightower's direct with "a ticking clock." Close cross of Cam Martinez with "you received a personal bonus for finishing early."
